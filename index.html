About Me

I first discovered coding in 5th grade when I had a boot cast and couldn’t go outside for recess. Stuck in the library, I stumbled upon Scratch on scratch.mit.edu, and that’s when my journey into programming began. The following year, in middle school, we had a tech class where we were introduced to Scratch—but I already knew the ins and outs of it. So, I decided to explore Python on my own. By 7th grade, our tech class focused on HTML, but I had already spent the summer teaching myself how to build websites. Over time, I expanded my skills to include CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL, constantly learning and building new projects along the way. My passion for coding has only grown, and I’m excited to continue developing my skills and creating new things.
